Hi, I'm Justin.

Thanks for taking the time to check out my portfolio.

I'm an experienced Game Designer turned Software Engineer currently living in Denver Colorado. Originally from Indiana, after graudating high school I moved far away from home to attend Full Sail University and pursue my dream of becoming a professional game developer. After earning my Bachelor's degree in 2005 I spent the next 11 years working on a wide variety of games in a number of different roles, moving my way up from QA Tester all the way to Lead Designer. During this time I was exposed to a range of different interesting design challenges and technologies such as motion controls, peripheral design and making the transition into mobile game development. These experiences have helped shape me into a skilled designer capable of taking on new challenges.

In 2016 I made the decision to transition into a Software Engineering role. I enjoy learning new things and working as a programmer this last year has given me an opportunity to develop and hone my technical skills. I continue to push myself as a developer and I've currently been focusing on learning different web development technologies and frameworks (which have helped me put together this portfolio site you're currently viewing!)

I enjoy making things that help people and make them happy, be it a game, tool, website or app. Most of my free time is spent building new things in hopes of continuing to learn and improve my skills. I also love exchanging ideas and experiences with others and meeting new people to "talk shop" with. Feel free to reach out if you're ever interested in discussing things like development or game design!
